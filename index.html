Good to Know

Frequently asked

How long does production take?+
Every piece is made to order, typically in 3–5 business days. If your date is close, choose a rush option at checkout and we'll prioritise your order.
Do you ship worldwide?+
Yes — we ship worldwide with full tracking on every order. Shipping is complimentary on orders over $150.
Will I see a proof before you make it?+
Always. For every personalized order we send a free digital proof for your approval before payment and production — nothing is cut until you're happy.
Can I order custom or bulk for my business?+
Absolutely. Restaurants, hotels and brands enjoy wholesale pricing and a saved brandbook for effortless reorders through our Trade Program.
